Strategic Recommendations

🚨 Priority 1: Decision-Making Systems (Next 30 Days)

Establish clear decision-making frameworks (RACI) and delegate authority appropriately. Critical organizational bottleneck affecting all leadership effectiveness. Expected outcome: 25% improvement in decision speed and clarity.

🎯 Priority 2: Ownership & Accountability Enhancement (Next 60 Days)

Implement advanced accountability frameworks and "Courageous Leadership" training program. Focus on decision ownership, difficult conversations, and clear expectation setting. Expected outcome: 22% improvement in Ownership & Accountability scores.

🛡️ Priority 3: Psychological Safety Enhancement (45-90 Days)

Implement regular pulse surveys and "failure celebration" sessions where teams share learnings without blame. Create anonymous feedback mechanisms and establish psychological safety training for all managers.

📈 Priority 4: Coach & People Developer Enhancement (60-120 Days)

Launch internal coaching certification program focusing on the Coach & People Developer dimension. Pair high-performing coaches with developing leaders using proven mentoring frameworks. Expected outcome: 30% improvement in team development metrics.

🏆 Priority 5: Performance & Growth Management Systems (90 Days)

Strengthen Performance & Growth Manager capabilities through enhanced recognition systems, goal alignment processes, and development planning tools. Create digital performance management platform with peer feedback mechanisms. Expected outcome: 25% improvement in performance management consistency.

📚 Priority 6: Continuous Leadership Development Culture (Ongoing)

Establish monthly leadership development sessions, quarterly leadership summits, and structured mentorship programs. Focus on reinforcing all 6 key leadership dimensions through peer learning, book studies, and best practice sharing.
