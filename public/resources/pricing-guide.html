üí° Pricing Psychology Tips

Always Present 3 Options

Option 1: Assessment Only ($97) - "Basic insight"

ROI Before Price

Always establish value before revealing price:

  1. "What would a 20% improvement in leadership effectiveness be worth?"
  2. Let them calculate and state the value
  3. Present your price as a fraction of that value

Payment Terms Strategy

  • Individual clients: Payment in full (builds commitment)
  • Corporate clients: 50% down, 50% on completion
  • Large projects: 3-payment structure (down, midpoint, completion)
