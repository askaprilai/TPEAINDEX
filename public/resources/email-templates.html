üìù Email Template Best Practices

  • Personalization: Always replace variables with specific details
  • Subject Lines: Keep under 50 characters for mobile optimization
  • Call to Action: Each email should have one clear next step
  • Timing: Send during business hours (Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM best)
  • Follow-up: Wait 3-4 days between follow-ups
  • Value First: Always provide value before asking for anything

Email Success Formula

Personalization + Value + Clear CTA = Response

Customize every email and focus on client value, not assessment features.
